How it works
Local Playback
Audio and video play directly from this machine via mpv. Connect HDMI to your display and audio to your amplifier.
Remote Control
Share the Remote URL with guests so they can search and add songs from their phone. They can't control playback or settings.

Idle Screen
When nothing is playing, the display shows a QR code so guests can easily find the remote URL and add music.
